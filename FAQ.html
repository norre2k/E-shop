Frequently asked questions

Does the item come with the keyboards displayed?

Answer: No, they do not. The keycap and or switch set on display is the only thing that will arrive at your post office.

When can I expect my item to be shipped?

After our quality assurance has been confirmed you can expect the item to ship within the next day from our warehouse.

Is it possible to refund the item?

Yes, it's possible to refund so long as we've not shipped the item in question.
